The Crow

Their flight style is unique, a patient, methodical flapping that is rarely broken up with glides.

  • Corvus albus
  • 1.8 - 2.6ft
  • 10-7 years
  • Open Woodlands

A crow is a bird of the genus Corvus, or more broadly a synonym for all of Corvus. The word "crow" is used as part of the common name of species including: Corvus albus, Corvus benniti, Corvus Capensis etc.
